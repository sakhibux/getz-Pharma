Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP)

GMP are guidelines that ensure pharmaceutical products are consistently produced and controlled according to quality standards. Key principles include:

  • Quality management principles
  • Controlling all stages of production and documentation
  • Preventing contamination and cross-contamination
  • Ensuring product safety and quality
  • Manufacturing process controls
  • Product safety and consistency
  • Equipment and facility hygiene

Good Storage Practices (GSP)

GSP focuses on maintaining the quality and integrity of pharmaceutical products during storage. Important aspects include:

  • Proper temperature and humidity control
  • Organized and systematic inventory management
  • Regular monitoring and documentation
  • Preventing product degradation
  • Proper storage conditions for pharmaceutical products

Compliance Requirements

All Getz Pharma employees must:

  • Undergo regular training on GMP and GSP
  • Follow documented procedures
  • Report any deviations immediately
  • Regulatory compliance
  • Standard operating procedures (SOPs)
  • Record-keeping practices
  • Quality assurance techniques
  • Risk management
  • Maintain personal hygiene and wear appropriate protective equipment
